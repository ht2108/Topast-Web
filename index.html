Our Story

ToPast began with a simple idea: what if we could transform the everyday toast experience into something extraordinary? Our founder, a pastry chef with over 15 years of experience, experimented with combining traditional pastry techniques with modern flavors.

Each ToPast creation is handcrafted with premium ingredients, from European-style butter to locally-sourced seasonal produce. We believe in quality without compromise, creating pastries that are as beautiful as they are delicious.
